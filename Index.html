Are you interested in programming?
Do you want to get better in programming?
Do you think you have the skills to win programming contests?
Then this is the club for you.

The ECHO club, formerly known as Computron, is where your skills in logic, abstract reasoning and decoding is at home. It doesn’t matter if you’re good at literature or good at memory, all it takes is a mind where you can see everything. And by everything, we mean every byte you encounter in every syntax you encode. From Scratch to Javascript, to C++ programming, ECHO is here to hone your skills.

How To Be A Member of Club ECHO

To become a member of ECHO, you must first take the qualifying exam. The exam composes of a practical and a written exam. The ones that will rank top, depending on the maximum required members for an ALA, will be accepted to the club.

Programmers Guild
Constitution and By-Laws

Constitution

Article I. Name
Section I. The organization shall be named as "ECHO" and shall be hereon referred to as "The Guild".

Article II. Membership
Section I. Membership in the organization will be through competitive examination, offered to all students of Philippine Science High School Eastern Visayas Campus. The highest scores from the admission test will be accepted into the organization.
Section II. The student should not have a grade lower than 2.5 in any subject

Article III. Purpose
Section I. This club aims to train amateurs and intermediates alike in the skill of programming, to train them for competitions, the NOI specifically.
Section II. This club hopes to create a social circle of programmers who can share their ideas and skills.

Article IV. Meetings
Section I. Regular meetings shall be held every ALA period, which is every Wednesday of the week, excluding holidays.
Section II. Special meetings can be held, which will be at the group’s discretion.
Section III. There will be no meeting on the week before the quarter exams, as it is mandated in the Student’s Handbook of the Philippine Science High School System.

Article V. Officers and Duties
Section I. The President will be in charge of overseeing all decisions and actions done within the group
Section II. The VicePresident will be in charge of assisting the President in related matters, as well as being officer inchargein the scenario of the President’s departure
Section III. The Secretary will be in charge of recording all activities, and documenting it, as well as arranging all of the meetings with the team.
Section IV. The Treasurer will be in charge of holding the funds to be used for matters related to The Guild.
Section V. The Public Information Officer will be in charge of disseminating information amongst the student body, as well as internally within The Guild, in cases of special meetings, or announcements.

Wondering why we are named ECHO, then find out after you become one of us. If you want to know more about ECHO then please feel free to navigate the page. For more questions, please consult Mr. Cary Von Alano in the Comp. Sci. Faculty.
